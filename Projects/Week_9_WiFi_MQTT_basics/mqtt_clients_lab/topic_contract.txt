Week 9 / 4.3 MQTT clients topic contract

Base namespace:
- iot_practice/USER_ID/#

Topics:
- iot_practice/USER_ID/lamp
  Payload: on | off

- iot_practice/USER_ID/lamp/mode
  Payload: dimmer | rgb

- iot_practice/USER_ID/lamp/value
  Payload: integer 0..100

- iot_practice/USER_ID/lamp/color
  Payload formats:
    - rgba(R,G,B,A), e.g. rgba(255,120,40,1.0)
    - HEX #RRGGBB, e.g. #FF7828

- iot_practice/USER_ID/status
  Payload example: {"source":"fake_lamp","mode":"rgb","lamp":"on"}

Compatibility rules:
- MQTTX desktop, fake_lamp, and mobile client must use identical topic names.
- Client apps should publish retained state for `lamp`, `lamp/mode`, and `lamp/value`.
